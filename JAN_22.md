## Question
## What would be the output of executing the following code:
## print(divmod(7, 3))
### A: (2, 1)
### B: (1, 2)
### C: (2.0), (1.0)
### D: (2.0, 1.0)
## Answer: A: (2, 1)
## Explanation: 
The built-in function divmod takes two arguments and returns a tuple of two values, the quotient and remainder.
